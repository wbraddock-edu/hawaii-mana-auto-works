About Mana Auto Works

Hilo's most trusted auto repair shop since 2010.

Est. 2010

Built on Trust, Driven by Expertise

Mana Auto Works was founded by master mechanic Kainoa Mana, a Big Island native who grew up watching his father work on every vehicle in their Hilo neighborhood. After 15 years working at dealerships on the mainland and earning his ASE Master Technician certification, Kainoa returned to Hilo with a vision: an auto shop that treats customers with respect, prices work honestly, and stands behind every repair.

The name Mana means "spiritual power" in Hawaiian — it reflects the values we bring to every repair: strength, integrity, and a deep connection to this community we serve.

Since 2010, we've grown from a two-bay shop to a full-service facility with 8 lifts, a tire center, and a team of 12 ASE-certified technicians. But our values have never changed: honest diagnostics, quality parts, and service you can trust.
