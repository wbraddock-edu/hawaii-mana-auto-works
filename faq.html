Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we hear from our customers.

Yes! We offer complimentary computer diagnostics on your first visit. This includes a full OBD-II scan and a basic visual inspection of your vehicle's major systems. On return visits, there's a $49.95 diagnostic fee that is credited back to you if you proceed with repairs. We believe you should understand what's wrong with your vehicle before you spend money — no surprises.
Most routine maintenance services (oil changes, tire rotations, brakes) are completed same-day, often within 1–3 hours. More complex repairs like transmission work or engine repairs may take 2–5 business days depending on parts availability. We always provide an estimated completion time at the time of drop-off, and we'll call you immediately if anything changes. We also offer loaner vehicles for qualifying repairs.
All parts and labor are covered by our 12-month/12,000-mile warranty — whichever comes first. As a NAPA AutoCare Center, we also offer the nationwide NAPA Peace of Mind warranty, which covers your repair at any NAPA AutoCare Center across the country. If a covered repair fails within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no charge.
Yes, we service all domestic, Asian, and European makes and models including Toyota, Honda, Nissan, Subaru, Ford, Chevy, GMC, Jeep, BMW, Mercedes, and more. Our technicians are trained on a wide range of vehicle systems, and we use professional-grade diagnostic equipment that covers all manufacturers' systems.
Hawaii's saltwater air dramatically accelerates corrosion on brake lines, exhaust systems, and undercarriage components. The constant heat and humidity affect rubber seals, hoses, and gaskets faster than temperate climates. The Big Island's lava roads can be particularly harsh on tires and suspension. We adjust our inspection checklists to focus on these Hawaii-specific issues and recommend more frequent checks on corrosion-prone components.
Yes, we partner with Snap Finance and Synchrony Bank to offer flexible payment options for repairs over $500. You can apply in minutes at the shop and often receive a decision immediately. There are no-interest options available for qualifying repairs paid within promotional periods. We want to make sure you can get the repairs you need without financial stress.
Yes! We have a secure after-hours key drop box at our Kilauea Avenue location. Simply fill out the service envelope, drop your keys, and our service advisor will call you first thing in the morning to confirm the services needed and provide an estimate before any work begins.
Yes — we offer emergency road service for customers in the Hilo area. If you're stranded on the side of the road, call us at (808) 555-0103. We'll do our best to dispatch a technician or arrange towing to get you to our shop. Emergency calls are subject to availability and a service call fee, which is credited toward any repairs performed at our shop.
